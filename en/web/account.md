# Account

Your GuildTags account is linked to your Discord profile. There is no separate registration — simply log in with Discord to create and use your account.

---

## Creating an account

GuildTags does not have a traditional registration form. Your account is created automatically the first time you log in via the Discord OAuth2 flow. To log in:

1. Go to [guildtags.com/tags](https://guildtags.com/tags)
2. Click **Login with Discord**
3. Authorise the GuildTags application on Discord's permission screen
4. You are redirected back to the site, now logged in

GuildTags only requests your public Discord profile — your username, avatar, and user ID. No access to your messages, server list, or private information is requested.

---

## What your account includes

Once logged in, your account allows you to:

- Browse the full directory of registered servers
- Like servers and track your liked servers
- Appear on the leaderboard as a voter
- Register and manage your own server's listing (if you are a server administrator)

---

## Logging out

To log out, click your user avatar or name in the top navigation bar and select Log out. Your session data is stored in your browser and is cleared on logout.

---

## Deleting your account

To request deletion of your GuildTags account and all associated data, contact the GuildTags team via the [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/9CqvbP6Yfx) or submit a request through the Privacy section. Account deletion includes:

- Your user record and login history
- Your likes and voting history
- Any server listings registered under your Discord account

Deletion requests are processed within 48 hours in accordance with the [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

---

## Security

GuildTags uses Discord's OAuth2 system for authentication. GuildTags never stores your Discord password or access tokens on its servers — only your public profile information. Your session is managed via a secure token stored in your browser's local storage.

If you believe your account has been compromised, revoke GuildTags' access from your [Discord Authorised Apps](https://discord.com/developers/applications) settings page and contact the GuildTags support team.
