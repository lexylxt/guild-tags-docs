# Leaderboard

The GuildTags leaderboard ranks all registered servers by the number of likes they have received from the community. It is a live ranking that updates as users like and unlike servers.

---

## Accessing the leaderboard

The leaderboard is at [guildtags.com/leaderboard](https://guildtags.com/leaderboard). It is publicly visible without logging in, though you must be logged in to cast a like.

---

## Leaderboard tabs

The leaderboard has two tabs:

**Most Liked** — a cumulative ranking based on the total number of likes each server has received since registration. This reflects overall community support over time.

**Most Voted** — a ranking based on recent activity. Servers that have received more likes in the current rolling period rank higher, regardless of their historical total.

---

## Podium

The top three servers on each tab are displayed on a podium:

- Second place is shown on the left
- First place is in the centre at the top
- Third place is on the right

Servers ranked 4th and beyond are shown in a scrollable list below the podium.

---

## How likes work

Each logged-in GuildTags user can like any server once. Likes are permanent until the user removes them. There is no expiry on likes — they count towards a server's total indefinitely.

To like a server, visit the [directory](/documentation/en/web/dashboard) and click the like button on any listing.

---

## Featured servers and the leaderboard

Featured servers (those with an active Featured plan) are pinned to the top of the directory but they compete fairly on the leaderboard based on their actual like count. Featured status does not grant any ranking advantage on the leaderboard.

---

## Getting your server on the leaderboard

Any server registered in the GuildTags directory automatically appears on the leaderboard. There is no separate submission process. As users discover and like your server, your ranking will rise.

To maximise your visibility:

- Make sure your server is registered and your tag is current
- Share your GuildTags listing link with your community
- Consider a Featured plan to gain top placement in the directory, which increases exposure
