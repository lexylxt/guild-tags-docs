# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

### What is GuildTags?

GuildTags is a public directory of Discord clan tags. Server owners register their tag and it becomes searchable and browsable on [guildtags.com](https://guildtags.com). Members of the community can discover new servers, like their favourites, and join directly from the directory.

---

### Is GuildTags affiliated with Discord?

No. GuildTags is an independent platform. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Discord Inc. Discord is a trademark of Discord Inc.

---

### Is GuildTags free to use?

Yes. Browsing the directory, liking servers, and registering your server are all free. The only paid feature is the Featured plan, which gives your server top placement in the directory.

---

## Tags

### My server does not have a clan tag. Can I still register?

No. A clan tag must be set on your Discord server before you can register with GuildTags. Clan tags are configured in your server's settings under the Clan section. If that section is not visible, your server may not yet meet Discord's requirements for the clan system.

---

### How long does it take for my tag to appear in the directory?

Registration is immediate. Your listing appears in the directory as soon as you run `/register` or submit through the website.

---

### I changed my Discord clan tag. Will GuildTags update automatically?

Yes. The bot syncs your tag periodically. If you want the update to appear immediately, run `/update` in your server.

---

### Can I have multiple servers listed?

Yes. Each Discord server is treated as a separate listing. If you administer multiple servers, you can register each one independently.

---

## Bot

### The bot is not responding to commands. What should I check?

First, verify that the bot is online (it should appear in your server's member list). Then check that it has the Use Application Commands permission in the channel you are using. If the issue persists, check the [GuildTags status page](/status) for any ongoing incidents.

---

### Do I need to keep the bot in my server after registering?

Yes. The bot handles automatic tag syncing. If you remove the bot, your listing will no longer receive updates and may become outdated. To remove your listing cleanly, run `/unregister` before removing the bot.

---

## Account

### I logged in but I cannot see the directory.

Make sure you completed the Discord authorisation step fully and were redirected back to guildtags.com. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser's cache. If the issue persists, try logging out and back in.

---

### How do I delete my account?

Contact the GuildTags team via the [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/9CqvbP6Yfx) or submit a data deletion request. All account data is removed within 48 hours.

---

## Leaderboard

### Why is my server not appearing on the leaderboard?

All registered servers appear on the leaderboard automatically. If your server is not showing, it may not be registered yet. Run `/status` in your server to check your registration state.

---

### Can I buy likes or boost my ranking?

No. Artificially inflating like counts through automation or multiple accounts violates GuildTags' terms of service and will result in the server being removed from the directory.
